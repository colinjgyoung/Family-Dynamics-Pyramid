Family Dynamics — Character Pyramid

Family Dynamics deck · facilitation exercise
Every family enterprise runs on a mix of behavioural patterns, not one type per person. This exercise asks you to rank the six cards that speak to you most — not to box yourself into a single label.
Read the cards below — click a card to flip it over. When one feels like you, hit Choose, then click a spot in the pyramid to place it. Top spot = your strongest match, the two below = next closest, the bottom three = the rest of your mix. The × icon on a placed card sends it back to the list. You don't have to fill all six — once you've placed three, a finish button appears below the cards. (You can also drag cards straight in, and the keyboard works too: Tab + Enter, Esc cancels.)
